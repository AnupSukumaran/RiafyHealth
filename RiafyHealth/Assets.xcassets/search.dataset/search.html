Here is the relationship between Vitamin D levels and Belly fat

A new study has found an interesting relationship between Vitamin D levels and Belly Fat. Low Vitamin D levels can result in poor bone health and increased risk for respiratory infection. “The strong relationship between increasing amounts of abdominal fats and lower levels of vitamin D suggests that individuals with larger waistlines are at a greater risk of developing deficiency, and should consider having their vitamin D levels checked,” the study author noted.
